Our Bot Detection Approach

Discover how we safeguard your platform with cutting-edge bot detection techniques.

Browser Fingerprinting

Detecting bots with browser fingerprinting involves analyzing unique browser and device characteristics to identify and track bot behavior based on inconsistencies or anomalies in the fingerprints.

Honey Pots

Detecting bots through honeypots involves setting up fake systems or services to attract and monitor malicious bots, revealing their behavior and identifying them based on their interactions.

Network Analysis

Bot detection via network analysis identifies unusual traffic patterns, such as irregular request rates and IP behavior, to distinguish bots from humans.

Behaviour Analysis

Bot detection through behavioral analysis identifies bots by spotting unnatural user actions, like repetitive movements or atypical interaction patterns, that differ from human behavior.
